Where's the crime in Saskatoon?

Inspired by the recent reports of Saskatoon having the highest crime rate in Canada, I decided to create a quick visualization of the neighbourhoods most affected by crime in the city. The map below charts the number of crimes per region in Saskatoon from the years 2004 to 2012. Darker regions are correlated to higher crime rates than lighter colored regions. You can adjust the year using the slider at the top of the map and hover over the map to view the raw data for each neighbourhood.

Crime data was obtained from the CommunityView Collaboration website. Neighbourhood map data was obtained from the City of Saskatoon's Open Data Catalogue. Exploratory analysis and data preparation was performed using R. Visualisation was done using d3.
